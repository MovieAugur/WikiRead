The year 2009 saw the release of many films. Seven made the [[List of highest-grossing films|top 50 list of highest-grossing films]]. Also in 2009, the [[Academy of Motion Picture Arts and Sciences]] announced that as of that year, their Best Picture category would consist of ten nominees, rather than five (the first time since the 1943 awards).

==2009 films==
Films released in North America in 2009 include:
* W: wide release (600 or more theaters)
* L: limited release in select cities (fewer than 600 theaters)
* R: re-release of previously released film
* ‡: films that achieved wide-release status after initial release
