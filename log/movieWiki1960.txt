The year '''1960 in film''' involved some significant events.
__TOC__

==Events==
*  March 5 - for the first time since coming home from military service in  Germany, [[Elvis Presley]] returns to [[Hollywood, California]] to film ''[[G.I. Blues]]'' 
* August 10 - Filming of ''[[West Side Story (film)|West Side Story]]'' begins.
* November 4 - Filming wraps on ''[[The Misfits (film)|The Misfits]]'', starring [[Marilyn Monroe]] and [[Clark Gable]] (this was the last film either performed in)
