The year '''1962 in film''' involved some significant events.

==Events==
* May - The [[Golden Horse Film Festival and Awards]] are officially founded by the Taiwanese government.
* August 5 - Hollywood Legend/Icon [[Marilyn Monroe]] is found dead of a [[Drug Overdose]]
* September 7 - Filming of [[Sergei Bondarchuk]]'s ''[[War and Peace (film series)|War and Peace]]'' begins and will continue for another 5 years.
* October 5 - ''[[Dr. No (film)|Dr. No]]'' launches the [[James Bond]] film series, the second longest-running motion picture franchise of all time (next to [[Godzilla]]), running 50 years. It also launched the film career of [[Sean Connery]].
