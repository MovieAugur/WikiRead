The year '''2000 in film''' involved some significant events.
The top grosser worldwide was ''[[Mission: Impossible II]]''. Domestically in North America, ''[[Gladiator (2000 film)|Gladiator]]'' won the Academy Awards for Best Picture and Best Actor ([[Russell Crowe]]).

There were also several popular action films, including ''[[X-Men (film)|X-Men]]'' and ''[[Shanghai Noon]]''. In the comedy realm, there was ''[[Me, Myself, and Irene]]'' and ''[[Meet the Parents]]''.

''[[Dinosaur (film)|Dinosaur]]'' was the most expensive film of 2000 and a box-office success.
