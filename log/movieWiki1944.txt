The year '''1944 in film''' involved some significant events, including the wholesome, award-winning ''[[Going My Way]]'' plus popular murder mysteries such as ''[[Double Indemnity (film)|Double Indemnity]]'', ''[[Gaslight (1944 film)|Gaslight]]'' and ''[[Laura (1944 film)|Laura]]''.

==Events==
*July 20 - ''[[Since You Went Away]]'' is released.
*September 6 - ''[[Double Indemnity (film)|Double Indemnity]]'' is released.

==Top grossing films (U.S.)<ref>''Box Office Digest'', March 1945, p. 18.</ref>==
Box office numbers were reported at the time as a percentage compared to "normal" business at each theater. For example, ''[[Going My Way]]'' performed at a massive 264% while ''[[To Have and Have Not (film)|To Have and Have Not]]'' was a hit at 173%. This is why exact dollar grosses for the period are unreliable at best.
