The year 1998 in film involved many significant films including; ''[[Shakespeare in Love]]'' (which won the [[Academy Award for Best Picture]]), ''[[Saving Private Ryan]]'', ''[[American History X]]'', ''[[The Truman Show]]'', ''[[Primary Colors (film)|Primary Colors]]'', ''[[Rushmore (film)|''Rushmore'']]'', ''[[Rush Hour (1998 film)|Rush Hour]]'', ''[[There's Something About Mary]]'', ''[[The Big Lebowski]]'', and [[Terrence Malick]]'s directorial return in ''[[The Thin Red Line (1998 film)|The Thin Red Line]]''. Animated films included ''[[A Bug's Life]]'', ''[[Antz]]'', ''[[Mulan (1998 film)|Mulan]]'' and ''[[The Prince of Egypt]]''.

[[Warner Bros.|Warner Bros. Pictures]] celebrated its 75th anniversary in 1998.
