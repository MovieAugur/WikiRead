==Events==
* [[Timeline of CGI in film and television|CGI technique]] is expanded with [[motion capture]] for [[Computer-generated imagery|CGI]] characters, used in ''[[Total Recall (1990 film)|Total Recall]]'' (1990).
* The first digitally-manipulated [[matte painting]] is used, in ''[[Die Hard 2]]''.
* The use of real-time [[computer graphics]] or "[[digital puppetry]]" to create a character in a motion picture is performed, in ''[[RoboCop 2]]''.
*Future movie stars [[Chris Rock]] and [[Adam Sandler]] join ''[[Saturday Night Live|SNL]]''.
*February 4 - Actor [[Tom Cruise]] and actress [[Mimi Rogers]] divorce.
*December 24 - Tom Cruise and actress [[Nicole Kidman]] marry.
