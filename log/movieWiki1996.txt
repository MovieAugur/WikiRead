The year 1996 involved many significant films. The major releases this year included ''[[Scream (1996 film)|Scream]]'', ''[[Independence Day (1996 film)|Independence Day]]'', ''[[Fargo (film)|Fargo]]'', ''[[Trainspotting (film)|Trainspotting]]'', ''[[The Rock (film)|The Rock]]'', ''[[The English Patient (film)|The English Patient]]'', ''[[Twister (1996 film)|Twister]]'', ''[[Mars Attacks!]]'', ''[[Jerry Maguire]]'' and a [[Evita (1996 film)|version]] of ''[[Evita (musical)|Evita]]'' starring [[Madonna (entertainer)|Madonna]].
