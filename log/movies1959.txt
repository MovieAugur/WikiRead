The year '''1959 in film''' involved some significant events, with ''[[Ben-Hur (1959 film)|Ben-Hur]]'' winning a record 11 [[Academy Awards]].

==Events==
* [[The Three Stooges]] make their 190th and last short film, ''[[Sappy Bull Fighters]]''.
*September 18 - [[The Marx Brothers]]' [[Zeppo Marx]] marries his second wife, [[Barbara Blakely]].
* November 18 - [[William Wyler]]'s film ''[[Ben-Hur (1959 film)|Ben-Hur]]'' premieres at [[Loews Theater]] in New York City.
* [[Sammy Davis, Jr.]] and [[May Britt]] start dating.
* The film of ''[[Mise Éire]]'', made by [[George Morrison (documentary maker)|George Morrison]] for [[Gael Linn]], is premiered at the [[Cork Film Festival]], the first feature-length [[Irish language]] film.
