== Events ==
* May 21 - ''[[The Empire Strikes Back|Star Wars Episode V: The Empire Strikes Back]]'' is released and is the biggest grosser of the year (just as its predecessor, ''[[Star Wars Episode IV: A New Hope|Star Wars]]'', was three years prior).
* June 20 - ''[[The Blues Brothers (film)|The Blues Brothers]]'' is released and in addition to becoming one of the top grossing films of the year it also became the first feature film to be based on characters created on ''[[Saturday Night Live]]''.
* November 19 - ''[[Heaven's Gate (film)|Heaven's Gate]]'' becomes one of the [[List of box office bombs|biggest box office bombs of all-time]] and its colossal failure bankrupted [[United Artists]].
