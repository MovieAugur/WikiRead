==Events==
* March 13 - The [[Dogme 95]] movement is officially announced in Paris by Danish directors [[Lars von Trier]] and [[Thomas Vinterberg]].
* June 10 - ''[[Pocahontas (1995 film)|Pocahontas]]'' has the largest premiere ever for a movie, premiering in [[Central Park]], [[New York City]] with an audience of 100,000, free admission.
* November 17 - After a six-year hiatus, the [[James Bond]] film series resumes with the successful ''[[GoldenEye]]'' with [[Pierce Brosnan]] as James Bond.
* November 22 - ''[[Toy Story]]'' is released debuting at the #1 and remains on the film charts for 18 weeks, marking the first feature-length film created completely using [[computer-generated imagery]].
